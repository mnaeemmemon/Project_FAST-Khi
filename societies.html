Our Socities

DECs

DECS has always been on the go in planning extra-curricular activities such as picnics and melas for the FASTians which prove to be both exhilarating and exclusive. The annual picnic, Drama Competition, Annual Study Tour, and the very formal Annual Dinner are some elite events organized by DECS which almost ALWAYS rock! This is the only official fan page of DECS. All the updates and news about the events of DECS will be shared here!...

The Literary Club

TLC is the most active, the most happening and the most classy society at FAST. With events like Xpressions, Zauq, Agha Hasan Abedi Declamation Contest, Intra-MUN, Parliamentary Debates and an annual magazine called the "localhost"; TLC tries to inspire a literary and artistic taste in the techies, geeks and nerds of FAST. TLC aims to turn the scientists of FAST into visionaries; because only true visionaries are able to say that “Code is poetry” and “Science is art”....

Think and Create

The objective of the committee is to develop within students, skill sets related to the industry. They shall be introduced to the current technical, managerial, ethical aspects of expertise in use or desired. Therefore, this is to ensure our fresh graduates to be more industry worthy, more confident in interviews. Moreover, TNC provides students with a resourceful platform for Projects and Research. This is to create an atmosphere, leading to new ideas and innovations with respect to the Industry. Hence, in order to achieve these objectives, the goals of ThinknCreate (TNC) is to organize - Seminars. - Workshops. - Motivational sessions. - Educational and Industrial Tours. - Alumni Meeting. - Showcase - Fastech - Recruitment Day ..

The WebMasters

TheWebMasters (TWM) is a student society at FAST-NUCES Karachi campus that is tasked with continous maintenance and enhancement of the campus website and managing its web presence. This group of highly talented and motivated students also conduct seminars, workshops, and competitions in the area of web design & development, database design, open source technologies among others....

The Character Building Society

CBS, it helps students improve themselves by showing a better character. We enable students to know and act on their responsibilities in different life roles....

ACM NUCES

The ACM Student Chapter at FAST-NUCES Karachi Campus is dedicated to the promotion of computing education, research and development. At ACM-NUCES KHI Chapter, you join a team that aims to change the methodology with which students approach computing and technology. We do our utmost to deliver the latest, and most innovative educational and professional development resources that our members require to strengthen their skill sets and enrich their careers. Most importantly, we take your view into consideration. At ACM-NUCES KHI Chapter. It’s all about YOU! Join Us and enjoy the following truly unique benefits: • Become a part of the energetic international ACM community. • Meet Entrepreneurs/Innovators leading Pakistan into a bright new future. • Learn from computing field's premier ACM Digital Library and • Expand your knowledge by using the ACM TechPack. • Take part in regional or international seminars and competitions. • Collaborate with the industry to work on the latest projects....
